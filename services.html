Our Services

At Zanema, we are committed to advancing the interests of employers in Zanzibar. Our expertise lies in providing comprehensive support and advocacy in industrial relations, employment practices, and business environment enhancement.

Practice Areas

We've Got Something for Every Employer

Industrial Relations

Expert guidance in managing employer-employee relations, ensuring compliance with labor laws, and resolving disputes effectively.

Employee Advocacy

Providing support for employers to understand and navigate employee rights and ensure fair treatment within the workplace.

Employment Law Compliance

Assistance in adhering to local employment laws and regulations to mitigate risks and foster a compliant workplace.

Policy Advocacy

Engaging with policymakers to represent employers' interests and influence legislation affecting the business environment.

Training & Workshops

Offering specialized training programs for employers on best practices in employment law, management, and workplace safety.

Conflict Resolution

Strategies and support for resolving workplace conflicts, fostering a positive working environment, and maintaining productivity.

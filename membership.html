Membership

ZANEMA Membership is open to all private employers, including individuals, partnerships, or companies, who wish to align with the goals and values of the Association.

Membership Categories

Honorary Member

Associate Membership

Membership Overview

Membership is open to all private employers, whether individuals, partnerships, or companies.

Honorary Members

The Management Committee may, at its discretion, invite any person or body corporate to be an honorary member of the Association as it deems fit.

Associate Membership

Any organization established by law and whose aims are similar to those of the Zanzibar Employers Association is eligible for admission as an Associate member.

Our Services

ZANEMA provides a wide range of services to support employers in Zanzibar, focusing on legal, advisory, and capacity-building areas essential for business success and compliance

Labor Relations & Dispute Resolution

Assisting employers in resolving labor disputes, ensuring fair labor practices, and fostering constructive workplace relationships.

Legal Advisory Services

Providing expert legal advice on labor laws and regulations to ensure businesses comply with national and international standards.

Employment Contracts & Policies

Helping employers draft and review employment contracts, workplace policies, and HR documents to safeguard their business and employees.

Workplace Health & Safety

Guiding employers on health and safety regulations, ensuring compliance with workplace safety standards for employee well-being.

Capacity Building & Training

Delivering training programs to improve employers' understanding of labor laws, dispute resolution, and other emerging issues in labor management.

Membership Services

Supporting our members through advocacy, networking opportunities, and the promotion of business-friendly policies in Zanzibar.

Advocacy for Business-Friendly Policies

Working with government and industry stakeholders to advocate for policies that support private sector growth and labor relations in Zanzibar.

Become a Member of ZANEMA

Why Join?

Becoming a member of ZANEMA provides you with exclusive access to legal support, business resources, and a network of industry leaders, all designed to help you succeed in Zanzibar's dynamic business environment.

Membership Benefits

  • Legal advisory services on labor laws and regulations
  • Support in labor relations and dispute resolution
  • Access to tailored training programs and workshops
  • Advocacy for business-friendly policies
  • Networking opportunities with industry leaders
  • Discounted services and exclusive member resources

How To Become a Member

Eligibility Criteria
  • private Employers: Business owners operating in Zanzibar, including small, medium, and large enterprises.
  • Entrepreneurs: Startups or new business owners seeking guidance and support in labor relations and compliance.
  • Professional Firms: Firms offering services to businesses and employers, such as legal, HR, or consulting firms.
Documents Required:
  1. Business Registration Certificate: Proof that your business is legally registered in Zanzibar.
  2. Tax Identification Number (TIN): A copy of your TIN issued by the relevant authorities.
  3. Proof of Employment: Documentation showing the number of employees in your business, such as payroll records or employee contracts.
  4. Business License: A valid business operating license, specific to your industry
  5. Articles of Association (if any): For incorporated businesses, which outlines the company’s structure and governance.
Membership Fees:
  • Membership fees vary depending on the size and type of your business. You can choose between:
    • Individual Membership: Suitable for small businesses and entrepreneurs.
    • Corporate Membership: Tailored for larger firms and organizations with more complex needs.

Application Process

Complete the Membership Application Form
  1. Complete the Membership Application Form

    2. Fill out our online membership application form with your business details. This form will require you to provide contact information, business type, and select the appropriate membership category.

  2. Submit Required Documents

    3. Upload the necessary documents during the application process. If needed, our support team will guide you through this step.

  3. Pay the Membership Fee

    4. After your application is reviewed, you'll receive an invoice for the membership fee. Payment can be made securely through our online payment portal.

  4. Receive Confirmation

    5. Once your application and payment are processed, you’ll receive a welcome package that includes your membership certificate and access to our member-exclusive benefits and resources.

Membership Renewal

Memberships are renewed annually. As a member, you will receive notifications when your renewal is due, and the process can be completed through our online portal.
